Website Summary:

What content will the site contain?

This site will contain information, fun facts, photos and stories about my two dogs, Blue and Tank. It will kind of be like a Wikipedia or fan page for them.

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

I am the source for all the content, I'll be using my personal photos of my dogs, along with my stories about them as the text.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

My site will enthrall the user with amazing stories and fun facts about why my dogs are amazing and should be honored.

Why should a site be built for this?

This site is essential to the continuation of mankind, everyone should visit this site to be educated on why my dogs are the best. Also because I miss my pets a lot and I think it will be a fun site to make.

What would you like users to do at your site?

I would like users to admire how adorable my dogs are and marvel at them, maybe even leave a comment on how cool they are.(hate comments will not be allowed on this website)

What is the goal of the site?

To share the glory of my pets and share something that brings me joy, so that others may also feel that same joy.
